<p><p>The alloy die castings market encompasses various casting methods tailored for different applications. Pressure die casting allows for high-volume production with precise dimensions using molten metal injected under pressure. Vacuum die casting minimizes gas porosity by creating a vacuum during the process. Squeeze die casting uses high pressure to enhance density and mechanical properties. Semi-solid die casting combines elements of casting and forging for improved material performance. Other methods include gravity die casting and low-pressure die casting, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>

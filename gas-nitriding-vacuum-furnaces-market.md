<p><p>The gas-nitriding vacuum furnaces market is primarily divided into two types: vertical and horizontal gas-nitriding vacuum furnaces. Vertical gas-nitriding furnaces utilize a vertical orientation, allowing efficient use of space and optimal gas flow, suitable for smaller components. In contrast, horizontal gas-nitriding furnaces support larger components and batch processing, providing ease of access and enhanced temperature uniformity. Both types are crucial for improving surface hardness and wear resistance in metals, catering to diverse industrial applications.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Single layer</li><li>Multi-layer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The anti-icing coating market is divided into single-layer and multi-layer types. Single-layer coatings consist of a single, protective film that prevents ice adhesion, offering a cost-effective solution with straightforward application. In contrast, multi-layer coatings involve multiple functional layers, each designed to enhance performance features like durability, anti-corrosion, and enhanced ice-repellency. While single-layer coatings are typically easier and quicker to apply, multi-layer options provide advanced protection, making them suitable for harsher environmental conditions and specialized applications.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Liquild Non-woven Filter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Meltblown</li><li>Needle Felt</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The liquid non-woven filter market comprises primarily two types: meltblown and needle felt. Meltblown filters are created by extruding molten polymer fibers that form a fine mat, providing excellent particle retention and high filtration efficiency, ideal for capturing small contaminants in liquids. In contrast, needle felt filters are produced by entangling fibers through needle punching, resulting in robust and durable materials suited for larger particles and higher flow rates. Both types serve critical roles in various industrial filtration applications.</p></p>

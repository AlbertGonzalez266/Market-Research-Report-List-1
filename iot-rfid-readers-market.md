<p><p>The IoT RFID readers market is categorized into three main types based on frequency: Low Frequency (LF) readers operate typically at 125-134 kHz and are ideal for applications like animal tracking and access control, offering short read ranges. High Frequency (HF) readers, usually at 13.56 MHz, support applications such as contactless payments and smart ticketing, with moderate read ranges. Ultra High Frequency (UHF) readers operate between 300 MHz and 960 MHz, enabling long-range reading suitable for supply chain management and inventory tracking.</p></p>

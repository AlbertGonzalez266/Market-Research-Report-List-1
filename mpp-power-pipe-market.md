<p><p>The MPP (Modified Polypropylene) Power Pipe market is segmented into two main types: excavation and non-excavation. The excavation type involves traditional digging methods to install or replace piping systems, typically requiring significant labor and equipment. In contrast, non-excavation methods use trenchless technologies, such as horizontal directional drilling, to install pipes with minimal surface disruption. This approach is often faster and less invasive, making it increasingly popular for urban infrastructure projects where soil disturbance needs to be minimized.</p></p>

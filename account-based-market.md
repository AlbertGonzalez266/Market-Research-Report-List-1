<p><p>Account-Based Marketing (ABM) comprises three main types: Strategic ABM, ABM Lite, and Programmatic ABM. Strategic ABM focuses on a small number of high-value accounts, crafting personalized campaigns tailored to each target's specific needs. ABM Lite targets a broader audience while maintaining some customization, balancing efficiency and personalization. Programmatic ABM automates targeting and engagement at scale, leveraging data and technology to reach a wide net of potential accounts with less individualized content. Each type serves different marketing objectives and resource allocations.</p></p>

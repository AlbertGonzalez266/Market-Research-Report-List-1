<p><p>The luminaire market encompasses various lighting technologies, including LED, CFL, LFL, HID, halogen, and incandescent luminaires. LED luminaires are energy-efficient and long-lasting, making them popular for modern applications. CFL (compact fluorescent lamps) offer decent energy savings but have a shorter lifespan than LEDs. LFL (linear fluorescent lamps) are common in commercial settings. HID (high-intensity discharge) luminaires are used for outdoor and industrial lighting due to their high output. Halogen luminaires provide crisp lighting, while incandescent luminaires, although less efficient, are favored for their warm glow.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Point Detector</li><li>Open Path Detector</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The infrared combustible gas sensors market is categorized into two main types: point detectors and open path detectors. Point detectors are compact devices that monitor specific locations for the presence of combustible gases, providing localized detection and rapid response to gas leaks. In contrast, open path detectors use a beam of infrared light to survey larger areas for gas concentrations, allowing for the detection of gas leaks over extended distances. Both types play crucial roles in safety and monitoring applications across various industries.</p></p>

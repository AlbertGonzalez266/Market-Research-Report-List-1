<p><p>The anti-corrosion valves market encompasses various types designed to withstand harsh environments. Ball valves utilize a spherical disc to regulate flow, ensuring tight sealing and resistance to corrosion. Plug valves feature cylindrical or tapered plugs that rotate to open or close the flow path. Butterfly valves employ a circular disc that pivots to control flow, offering quick operation and space efficiency. Other types include globe valves and check valves, each providing unique benefits for corrosion resistance in diverse industrial applications.</p></p>

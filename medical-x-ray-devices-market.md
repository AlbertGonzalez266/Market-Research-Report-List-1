<p><p>The medical X-ray devices market is primarily divided into two types: analog X-ray devices and digital X-ray devices. Analog X-ray systems use traditional film to capture images, resulting in longer processing times and less image manipulation flexibility. In contrast, digital X-ray devices employ electronic sensors to capture images quickly, allowing for immediate viewing and advanced post-processing options. Digital systems offer improved image quality, reduced radiation exposure, and enhanced workflow efficiency, driving their growing adoption in healthcare settings.</p></p>

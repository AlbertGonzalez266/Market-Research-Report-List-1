<p><p>The indoor rower market features various types, each catering to different preferences. Air rowers utilize fan resistance, providing a realistic experience but with noise. Magnetic rowers use magnets for smoother, quieter operation, ideal for home use. Hydraulic rowers, usually compact and affordable, offer resistance through hydraulic cylinders, suited for small spaces. Water rowers replicate the feel of rowing on water, using a water-filled tank for resistance, appealing for their aesthetic and immersive experience. Each type has distinct advantages for diverse users.</p></p>

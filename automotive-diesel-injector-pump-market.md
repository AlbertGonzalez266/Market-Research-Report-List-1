<p><p>The automotive diesel injector pump market comprises several types, each serving specific engine needs. The Common Rail Diesel Injection Pump allows multiple injectors to draw fuel from a single rail, enhancing efficiency. In-Line Diesel Injection Pumps feature a series of pumps lined up to deliver fuel directly to injectors, suitable for heavy-duty applications. Distributor (Rotary) Diesel Injection Pumps distribute fuel to multiple cylinders in a compact design, while Others encompass various niche designs and specialized pumps catering to unique engine configurations.</p></p>

<p><p>The noncontact temperature measurement market encompasses various technologies, primarily infrared temperature measurement and optical fiber temperature measurement. Infrared temperature measurement detects thermal radiation emitted by objects, allowing for quick, accurate readings from a distance, ideal for applications in industry and healthcare. Optical fiber temperature measurement employs fiber optic cables that sense temperature changes through light transmission, offering high sensitivity and immunity to electromagnetic interference. Both methods ensure safety and efficiency in environments where traditional contact methods are impractical or hazardous.</p></p>

<p><p>The water filters and purification market encompasses various technologies, primarily focusing on Reverse Osmosis (RO) and Ultrafiltration (UF). RO systems employ a semipermeable membrane to remove contaminants, providing highly purified water, ideal for residential and industrial use. UF, on the other hand, utilizes larger pore membranes to eliminate suspended particles, bacteria, and some viruses, making it suitable for pre-treatment or standalone filtration. Both methods cater to increasing health and safety concerns regarding water quality, driving market growth.</p></p>

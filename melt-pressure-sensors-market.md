<p><p>Melt pressure sensors are crucial in monitoring the pressure of molten materials during processing. NaK filled melt pressure sensors utilize a sodium-potassium alloy that provides a wide operational temperature range and high sensitivity, making them ideal for demanding applications. Conversely, oil-filled melt pressure sensors use a specific oil to transfer pressure signals, offering stability and reliability in measurement. Both types are essential in industries like plastics and rubber, ensuring optimal processing conditions and product quality.</p></p>
